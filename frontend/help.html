🚀 Introduction

VectorizeAI is a premium RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) platform. We enable developers to transform static data—PDFs, URLs, YouTube videos—into dynamic, conversational intelligence interfaces in seconds.

✨ Key Capabilities
  • Smart Vectorization: Automatic semantic chunking for deep data understanding.
  • Multi-LLM Engine: Connect Gemini, GPT-4, Claude, or local Ollama instances.
  • Built-in Auth: Enterprise-ready client management and API key scoping.
  • High Performance: Optimized retrieval pipeline with < 1s latency targets.

⚡ Quick Start

  1. Create Account: Head to Login and verify your email via OTP.
  2. Get API Key: Navigate to the Dashboard and generate your first infrastructure key.
  3. Configure Brain: In the Playground, apply your preferred LLM provider.
  4. Index Data: Upload your documents or paste a URL to begin vectorization.
  5. Chat: Start querying your private knowledge base immediately.

🔍 Troubleshooting

💡
Connection Issues? Verify your DATABASE_URL and ensure your AI Provider keys have active credits.
Common Solutions
  • OTP not arriving? Check your spam folder or verify SMTP settings.
  • 401 Unauthorized? Ensure the X-API-Key header is present in your requests.
  • Slow Responses? Consider switching to the Gemini Flash provider for faster generation.
